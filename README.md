# 📊 Marketplace Performance Dashboard

An interactive business intelligence dashboard that analyses marketplace sales performance using the **UCI Online Retail** dataset. This project demonstrates how data analytics and visualization can help businesses monitor performance, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, KPI development, and interactive dashboards.

The dashboard enables users to monitor:

- Revenue performance
- Sales trends
- Customer behaviour
- Product performance
- Geographic distribution of sales

The objective is to demonstrate practical Business Intelligence and Business Analytics skills using Excel and Power BI.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers

- Units Sold

### Sales Analysis

- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Country
- Top Performing Products
- Top Customers
- Sales by Month

### Interactive Filters

- Country
- Month

- Product Category (if applicable)

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Online Retail

Source:
https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/online+retail

Description:

- UK-based online retailer
- December 2010 – December 2011
- 500,000+ transactions
- Customer purchases from multiple countries

---

## 🔍 Business Questions

This dashboard answers questions such as:

- How is revenue changing over time?
- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which countries contribute the most revenue?
- Who are the highest-value customers?
- What are seasonal sales patterns?
- How many unique customers and orders were recorded?

---

## 📊 Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned before analysis by:

- Removing cancelled transactions
- Removing missing Customer IDs
- Handling invalid quantities
- Creating Total Sales column
- Standardising date formats

## 💡 Key Insights

- Identified best-selling products contributing the highest revenue.
- Analysed customer purchasing behaviour.
- Identified seasonal sales trends.
- Compared sales performance across countries.
